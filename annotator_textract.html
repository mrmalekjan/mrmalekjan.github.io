AI Papers Annotation for NLP Graph Construction

Duration: December 2021 - May 2022

Role: AI Papers Annotator

Project Overview

As part of an NLP research team, I annotated hundreds of artificial intelligence research papers to extract structured data for building knowledge graphs. The project aimed to create a comprehensive graph database that could reveal relationships between AI concepts, methodologies, and applications for advanced NLP analysis.

Annotation workflow for extracting structured data from research papers

Technical Skills Acquired

Responsibilities, Tasks and Projects

  • Analyzed and annotated 300+ AI research papers from top conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ACL)
  • Extracted key entities including: algorithms, datasets, evaluation metrics, and research domains
  • Identified relationships between concepts (implements, compares, improves, extends)
  • Maintained annotation consistency across the entire dataset with 95% inter-annotator agreement
  • Collaborated with NLP researchers to refine annotation guidelines based on emerging patterns
  • Participated in weekly quality assurance meetings to ensure data integrity

Outcomes & Impact

  • Created a knowledge graph of 15,000+ entities and 45,000+ relationships from AI literature
  • Enabled researchers to query relationships between AI concepts for literature reviews
  • Identified research trends and gaps in the AI landscape
  • Supported development of recommendation systems for relevant papers
  • Dataset used to train graph neural networks for automated paper analysis
